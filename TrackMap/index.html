How to Use This Map

  • The globe automatically rotates - use the "Stop Rotation" button in the top-left to pause/resume
  • Click any flag in the top row to zoom to that circuit
  • Click any marker (flag icon) on the map to see circuit details
  • Use the "Reset Zoom" button in the top-right to return to the global view
  • Zoom in close to a circuit to see its track layout and racing direction
