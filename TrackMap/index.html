About This Map

Welcome to the Formula 1™ 2025 World Championship Circuit Map. This interactive visualization showcases all 24 circuits that will host Grand Prix races in the 2025 season, from the season opener in Melbourne to the championship finale in Abu Dhabi.

Each circuit is represented by its national flag, and detailed information is available including:

  • First Grand Prix held at the circuit
  • Number of laps in the race
  • Circuit length
  • Circuit altitude
  • Current lap record

Data sources: Formula 1™, Formula 1™ circuits repository

How to Use This Map

  • The globe automatically rotates - click "Stop Rotation" in the top-left corner to pause/resume
  • On desktop: Hover over flags in the top bar to see circuit details
  • Click any flag marker on the map to see detailed circuit information in a popup
  • Zoom in close to a circuit to see its track layout and racing direction
  • Click "Reset Zoom" in the top-right corner to return to the global view
